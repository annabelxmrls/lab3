Let's explore mouse events!

Display Mouse Position

As the mouse moves around a specific area and clicks, the mouse's position will be displayed for the user to see. Try it out and click anywhere on the image of the meadow below!

You may also look at the console for additional information!

Trigger Animations

When the mouse clicks on a specific element, an animation is triggered and it usually involves a styled element moving to the position where the mouse clicked. Let's add a butterfly to the meadow by clicking on the image below. Then, click around the meadow to make the butterfly fly around! Reminder to look at the console log as well!

More Trigger Animations!

You can also trigger other animations with other ways of using your mouse. First, you can hold down the mouse over the daytime landscape image to see it as a nighttime image. Don't forget to look at the console log too!

Now try double clicking over the nighttime image below to turn it into the daytime image!
